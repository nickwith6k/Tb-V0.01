How to Use
Setting up your bot:
  1. Message your bot on Telegram
  2. Use /start to begin
  3. Configure your trade with /set_pair, /set_side, etc.
  4. Set your take profits and stop loss
  5. Use /place to execute your trade plan
Key commands:
  • /help - Show all commands
  • /status - Current configuration
  • /set_dryrun on - Enable test mode
  • /place - Start trading
  • /cancel - Cancel active trade
Safety First: Always test your configuration in dry-run mode before trading with real funds.
